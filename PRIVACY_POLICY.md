# Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-06

RailSeatExchange ("we", "our", "us") provides a train seat exchange platform for Indian Railways passengers. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## Information We Collect

1. Phone number
- Used for account creation and login via Firebase Phone Authentication.

2. Profile information
- Name provided during registration.

3. Exchange listing data
- Journey details you submit, such as train number, date, boarding station, destination, coach, berth preference, and related listing metadata.

4. Technical data
- Basic server logs and request metadata for security, fraud prevention, and service reliability.

## How We Use Information

We use collected information to:
- Authenticate users and secure accounts.
- Create and manage seat exchange listings.
- Match users for possible exchanges.
- Prevent abuse, fraud, and unauthorized access.
- Operate, maintain, and improve app performance.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data.

We may share limited data with service providers required to operate the app:
- Firebase Authentication (Google) for phone number verification.
- Cloud hosting provider (Render) for backend operation and storage.

We may also disclose data when required by law or valid legal process.

## Data Retention

We retain account and listing data as long as needed to provide the service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. You may request deletion as described below.

## Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your data. However, no system can be guaranteed 100 percent secure.

## Children

This app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

## Your Choices and Rights

You can:
- Stop using the app at any time.
- Request account and associated data deletion by contacting us.
