How it works

  1. Select a seat – click any booked (blue) seat to pick the seat you currently hold.
  2. Choose a target – click another booked seat to request an exchange.
  3. Confirm – both seats swap instantly in this demo.
  4. Click a green seat to book it as a new passenger.
